Otsukare (good work) is a free language learning game designed to help English speakers practice and become more confident in Japanese.

This website is designed to complement the popular language learning app Duolingo. We encourage you to practice exercises both here and on Duolingo. This app includes topics and special technology beyond what Duolingo currently offers, including..

  • More spoken and comprehension content
  • More focus on vocabulary memorisation
  • More sentences through random generation, including handling money, asking for things (needs, wants, usage)
  • Learning conversational elements like umms, ahhs, acknowledgements and asking to repeat
  • Specialised spaced repetition learning system that focuses your attention on mistakes made by yourself and other users
